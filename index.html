Welcome to StreamNexus –
Revolutionizing Classroom Learning

Imagine a world where education has no boundaries. With StreamNexus, lecturers can seamlessly live-stream their classes to multiple classrooms, ensuring that every student receives the same high-quality learning experience—no matter where they are.

🚀 Our Goal: To bridge the gap between physical and digital learning, making education more accessible, interactive, and efficient.

🎥 How It Works

  • Lecturers live-stream their lessons to other classrooms in real-time.
  • Students can watch, learn, and engage through smart boards.
  • A low-latency system ensures smooth, uninterrupted learning.

📚 Why StreamNexus?

  • ✔ Seamless Live-Streaming – No expensive setups, just quality education.
  • ✔ Better Connectivity – Leverage LAN-based streaming for ultra-low lag.
  • ✔ Effortless Classroom Management – Control and interact with students remotely.
