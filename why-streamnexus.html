Why Choose STREAMNEXUS

STREAMNEXUS was built from the ground up specifically for academic environments. Here's why it's better suited for education than general-purpose platforms like Zoom or Google Meet:

Designed for Classrooms

No distractions like personal chats or external participants. Our interface is focused on learning, not socializing.

Simple and Lightweight

Works on low-end devices without lag. Our platform is optimized for educational content delivery, not corporate meetings.

Easy Access

Simple class codes instead of long meeting links. Students can join with a 6-digit code rather than navigating complex URLs.

Built-in Notes & Recordings

Students can take notes and review recorded lectures directly within the platform, creating a seamless learning experience.

No Installations Required

Runs entirely in the browser without downloads or installations. Access education instantly from any device.

Education-First Security

Privacy controls designed for classroom settings. Only authorized students can access streams with proper authentication.
